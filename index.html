Trips


Podilsky Tovtry


National Park "Podilsky Castles" - a national park located on the territory of Ukraine \ t Gorodok, Chemerovetsky and Kamyanets-Podilsky districts of the Khmelnytsky region.

Shipit


Shipit- a waterfall in the Ukrainian Carpathians, on the northern slopes of the mountain range Polonin Borzhava, at the foot of Mount Gemba; hydrological nature monument of local significance.

Picu


Picu is the highest peak of the Dividing, or Verkhovyna Range, which is a continuation of the Besschad Strait located in Poland. Situated on the border of Lviv and Transcarpathian regions. A popular summer and winter tourism destination.

Polonina Borzhava


Polonina Borzhava is a mountain range on the Poloninsky Range in the Ukrainian Carpathians, between the rivers Vicha and Rika. Located within Volovetsky, Mizhhirsky, Svalyavsky, Irshavsky and (partly) Khust districts of the Transcarpathian region.

Synevyr


Lake Synevyr (other names are Synevir, Sea Eye) is the largest lake in the Ukrainian Carpathians. Located in the Mezhgirsky district of Zakarpattya the region, in the mountain range of Inner Gorgany. It is part of the Synevyr National Nature Park.

Pip Ivan


Pip Ivan is one of the highest peaks of the Ukrainian Carpathians, with a height of 2020.5 m. It is located on the border of Ivano-Frankivsk and Transcarpathian regions. A popular summer and winter tourism destination.
